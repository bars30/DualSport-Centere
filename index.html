Frequently Asked Questions

What are the membership hours?

Our center is open from 6 AM to 10 PM, seven days a week.

Are there any age restrictions for memberships?

No, our center is family-friendly and welcomes members of all ages.

Can I upgrade my membership plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade your membership at any time. Contact our support team for assistance.

Are there any special programs for kids?

Yes, we offer junior programs for kids and teens, including beginner classes and development programs in both tennis and golf.

What should I bring to my first class?

Please bring comfortable athletic wear, appropriate footwear, and any personal sports equipment you have. We provide rental equipment if needed.

How can I book a court or tee time?

You can book a court or tee time by calling our front desk.

Do you offer any discounts for long-term memberships?

Yes, we offer a 10% discount on annual memberships paid upfront.

What is included in the private lessons?

Private lessons include one-on-one coaching tailored to your skill level and goals. You can also schedule lessons at times that suit your availability.
