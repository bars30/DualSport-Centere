Welcome to DualSport Tennis & Golf Center

Our Story

Founded in 2021, DualSport Tennis & Golf Center was established with the goal of creating a premier sports facility in Armenia, specializing in both tennis and golf. Our founders envisioned a place where athletes of all ages and skill levels could come together to improve their game, enjoy state-of-the-art facilities, and be part of a thriving sports community.

From the beginning, our mission has been to promote the love of tennis and golf through top-quality training, modern amenities, and a supportive environment. Our center features cutting-edge tennis courts and a professionally designed golf course, providing the perfect setting for both beginners and advanced players to practice and excel.

We are committed to fostering a culture of excellence, sportsmanship, and continuous improvement. Our team of certified coaches brings years of experience and a passion for teaching, ensuring that every member receives personalized instruction and guidance. Whether you are a young athlete starting your journey or an experienced player aiming to refine your skills, DualSport Tennis & Golf Center offers programs tailored to meet your needs.

Since our inception, we have proudly hosted numerous tournaments, events, and community activities, creating lasting memories and friendships along the way. We believe that sports have the power to bring people together, and we strive to create an inclusive and welcoming environment for all our members.

At DualSport Tennis & Golf Center, we are more than just a sports facility; we are a community dedicated to the growth and enjoyment of tennis and golf. Join us and be part of our story as we continue to build a legacy of sporting excellence in Armenia.

Our Vision

To be the leading sports center in Armenia, inspiring and nurturing the next generation of tennis and golf enthusiasts. We aim to set the standard for excellence in sports training and facilities, creating a dynamic and inclusive environment where athletes can thrive. Our vision extends beyond just being a place to play; we aspire to be a hub of sports innovation, community engagement, and lifelong learning.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional training, facilities, and community support for players of all ages and skill levels, fostering a love for tennis and golf. Our mission is grounded in the belief that every athlete deserves the best opportunities to develop their skills and enjoy their sport. We are committed to:

  • Quality Coaching: Offering personalized instruction from certified coaches who are dedicated to helping each player reach their full potential.
  • State-of-the-Art Facilities: Maintaining world-class tennis courts and a professional olf course that meet the highest standards of excellence.
  • Community Engagement: Creating a vibrant community where members can connect, share their passion for sports, and build lasting friendships.
  • Inclusive Programs: Providing a wide range of programs tailored to all skill levels, from beginners to advanced athletes, ensuring that everyone can find their place at DualSport.
  • Continual Improvement: Continuously enhancing our offerings and facilities based on the latest advancements in sports science and technology.
  • Promoting Sportsmanship: Encouraging values such as respect, integrity, and teamwork in all our activities, fostering a positive and supportive environment.

At DualSport Tennis & Golf Center, our vision and mission guide everything we do. We are dedicated to creating an unparalleled sports experience that inspires and nurtures the next generation of tennis and golf enthusiasts in Armenia and beyond. Join us in our journey to excellence and be a part of our thriving sports community.
